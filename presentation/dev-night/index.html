What is a freelancer?

A freelancer is a self-employed person who often works on several projects for multiple clients at any one time

Full-Time job vs. Freelancing
Location Office based Remote / Non-location
Working hours 8/9am - 5/6pm (30 - 40 h/w) [X]am - [X]pm (0 - 100 h/w)
Freedom Follows Utmost freedom (!)
Contract Long-term Long-term / Short-term
Learning curve at work Mediocre level Higher learning curve. High productivity
Compensation Regular paycheck each month, year-end raise Non-steady paycheck. You work more, you earn more
Benefits Health care, pension plan, and paid time off... Typically, no benefits nor bonuses
